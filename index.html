What is XP: Reborn?

XP: Reborn is a community-driven project dedicated to preserving and extending the life of Windows XP. Our mission is to provide modern software compatibility, security updates, and essential tools for users who want to continue using this beloved operating system.

Our Goals

  • Curate compatible software for Windows XP systems
  • Provide security-focused browser alternatives
  • Maintain a collection of essential productivity tools
  • Support the retro computing community

Join us in keeping the classic Windows XP experience alive for enthusiasts, retro gamers, and legacy system users.

Meet the XP: Reborn Team

Our dedicated team of volunteers works tirelessly to keep Windows XP alive and thriving. We're passionate about preserving computing history and supporting the retro computing community.
